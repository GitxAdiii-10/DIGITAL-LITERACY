**Social Media Do's and Don t's for college students**\
\
\
\
The Do's\
\
1.Verify information before sharing Cross-check facts using reliable
sources to avoid spreading misinformation.\
**2.Protect your privacy** Adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing
sensitive personal information (e.g., address, phone number).\
**3.Think before you post** Consider the long-term impact of your
content on your reputation and digital footprint.\
**4.Use respectful and inclusive language** Communicate thoughtfully and
be mindful of diverse perspectives.\
**5.Recognize credible sources** Follow trusted organizations, experts,
and verified accounts.\
\
\
**The Dont's**\
\
**1.Don't spread misinformation or rumors** Sharing unverified content
can mislead others and cause harm.\
**2.Don't overshare personal details** Avoid posting sensitive
information that could be misused.\
**3.Don't engage in cyberbullying or trolling** Harmful online behaviour
can have serious consequences.\
**4.Don't click on suspicious links** These may lead to scams, phishing
attacks, or malware.\
**5.Don't blindly trust everything you see online** Not all content is
accurate, even if it looks professional.
