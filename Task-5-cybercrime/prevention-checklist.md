

## Part B – Prevention Checklist
'Stay Safe Online' Checklist
•Never enter a UPI PIN to receive money: Authenticate payments only when you are
sending money, as receiving requires no PIN.
•Verify QR code requests: Be extremely cautious of individuals sending QR codes to
"verify" your account or "credit" a payment.
•Use Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): Enable an extra layer of security on all social
media and email accounts.
•Secure Public Wi-Fi usage: Avoid logging into bank accounts or making purchases while
connected to campus or public Wi-Fi.
•Audit App Permissions: Regularly check which apps have access to your contacts,
location, and SMS.
•Identify Phishing Links: Do not click on unsolicited links in SMS or WhatsApp messages
claiming "KYC expired" or "Lottery won".
•Practice Password Hygiene: Use unique, complex passwords for your university portal and
personal banking.
•Verify Official Websites: Ensure websites use https:// and check for small misspellings
in the URL before entering data.
